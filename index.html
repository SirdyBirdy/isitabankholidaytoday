Is Today a Bank Holiday?

A bank holiday is an officially recognised public holiday when banks, government offices, and most businesses close. In India, national bank holidays include Republic Day (Jan 26), Independence Day (Aug 15), Gandhi Jayanti (Oct 2), plus a long list of state-level holidays. The UK gets 8 bank holidays a year. The US gets 11 federal holidays. Germany gets 9–13 depending on the state.
